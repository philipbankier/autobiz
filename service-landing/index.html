How does the 48-hour delivery work?

Once you pay and describe your idea (a short form), a team of 6 AI agents starts working immediately. A CEO agent creates the business strategy, a developer builds the product, a marketing agent creates your brand assets. You'll get updates as they work, and a review call before we go live.

Do I own everything?

100%. The code, the domain, the Stripe account, the content — it's all yours. We hand over complete ownership. No vendor lock-in, no ongoing fees unless you want continued AI agent operations.

What if I don't like what the agents built?

You get to review everything before launch. We'll iterate on feedback until you're happy. If we genuinely can't deliver what you need, full refund — no questions asked.

What's "1 week of AI agent operations"?

After launch, AI agents continue working: publishing social media content, responding to inquiries, monitoring analytics, and suggesting improvements. It's like having a virtual team running your business for a week while you focus on customers.

What tech stack do you use?

Modern web technologies: React/Next.js frontend, deployed on Vercel (fast, free hosting). Stripe for payments. Professional-grade code that you can extend or hand off to any developer later.
